Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 Aug 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how we (“90 Surge”, “we”, “us”) collect, use, and share information when you participate in our show-night raffle at 90surge.com (or our event site).

Information We Collect

  • Name/Nickname: you enter this to join the raffle.
  • Follow verification: when you tap our Facebook/Instagram buttons, we record that action; if you use Facebook Login to verify a Page like, Facebook may share a token confirming whether you like our Page (we do not receive your password).
  • IP address: stored temporarily to enforce “one entry per device” and to track whether a device has followed within the current show window.
  • Local storage (on your device): we set lightweight keys such as userName, followed:<windowKey>, and shownWinnerName:<windowKey> to manage UI state.
  • Operational logs: basic server logs for reliability and abuse prevention.

How We Use Your Information

  • Run the raffle (verify eligibility, prevent duplicate entries, pick and display a winner).
  • Show follower counts and follow status (aggregated).
  • Secure and operate the site (detect abuse, troubleshoot issues).

Retention

  • Follow status by IP: up to 8 hours (auto-expires).
  • Raffle entries: kept for the current show window and cleared after the event or by admin action.
  • Winner: stored until the winner is reset/cleared.
  • Local storage: stays on your device until you clear your browser data.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with service providers strictly to run the site (e.g., hosting, databases, content delivery). We also use Facebook/Instagram services to display our profiles and verify a follow/like when you choose to do so.

Cookies & Local Storage

We use local storage and similar technologies to keep your name on the form, remember follow status for the current show, and avoid re-showing the winner modal.

Legal Bases (EEA/UK)

Our processing is based on legitimate interests (running a fair raffle and operating a secure site) and, where applicable, your consent (e.g., Facebook Login).

Your Choices & Rights

  • You can clear your browser’s local storage/cookies at any time.
  • You can request access, correction, or deletion of your raffle data (see Data Deletion).

Data Deletion

To delete your raffle information, email us at privacy@90surge.com with:

  • Subject: “Data Deletion – 90 Surge Raffle”
  • Your name/nickname used to enter
  • Approximate date and venue of the show

We will confirm deletion of any server-side records associated with your entry for that show. (Note: you can also clear your own local storage anytime via your browser settings.)

Children’s Privacy

Our raffle is intended for general audiences at live shows. If you believe a minor provided us data, contact us and we’ll delete it.

Changes

We may update this Policy. We’ll post the new date at the top and, where appropriate, notify you by updating the event page.
